# Research Notes: Big Little Business Website

## Multi-Brand Website Best Practices

### Key Takeaways from Research:

1. **Centralized Management** - A multi-brand website enables centralized management across all brands, simplifying coordination for marketing, content creation, and operational tasks.

2. **Consistent Branding** - Brand consistency can increase revenue by 10-20%. The website must balance consistent branding across the portfolio with the need to differentiate each brand.

3. **Intuitive Navigation** - Website users spend an average of 6.44 seconds focused on the main navigation menu. Ensuring intuitive and user-friendly navigation is essential.

4. **Personalization** - 75% of consumers are more likely to buy from a retailer that includes some sort of personalization.

5. **Content Strategy** - 90% of marketers include content in their marketing strategies. A cohesive content strategy ensures a consistent user experience while allowing for brand-specific tailoring.

### Multi-Brand Website Examples to Reference:
- **Adobe** - Showcases Creative Cloud, Document Cloud, Experience Cloud under one umbrella
- **Intuit** - Features QuickBooks, TurboTax, Mint, Mailchimp
- **Microsoft** - Presents Office 365, Azure, Windows, Xbox
- **Oracle** - Multiple enterprise solutions under one brand

## High-Converting SaaS Website Elements

### Essential Components:
1. **Clear Value Proposition** - Immediately communicate what the company does
2. **Product Showcase** - Visual representation of each product
3. **Social Proof** - Testimonials, case studies, client logos
4. **Clear CTAs** - "Book a Demo", "Start Free Trial", "Contact Sales"
5. **Trust Indicators** - Security badges, compliance certifications
6. **Pricing Transparency** (if applicable)
7. **FAQ Section** - Address common objections

### Conversion-Focused Design:
- Above-the-fold hero with clear CTA
- Product cards with individual CTAs
- Testimonials from recognizable organizations
- Case studies showing ROI
- Easy contact/demo booking

## Color Scheme Notes

### Brand Colors:
- **Black** - Professional, authoritative, sophisticated
- **Gold** - Premium, trustworthy, successful

### Recommended Palette:
- Primary: Deep charcoal/black for text and accents
- Accent: Gold for CTAs, highlights, and branding elements
- Background: Clean white/off-white for readability
- Secondary: Warm grays for sections and cards
- Each product could have a subtle color identifier while maintaining overall brand consistency

## Website Structure Recommendation

### Pages:
1. **Home** - Hero, product overview, value proposition, testimonials, CTA
2. **Products** (or individual product pages) - Detailed features for each SaaS
3. **About** - Company story, mission, team
4. **Contact** - Form, phone, email

### Home Page Sections:
1. Hero with tagline and primary CTA
2. Product showcase (3 products with brief descriptions)
3. Why Choose Us / Value proposition
4. Client testimonials / Social proof
5. Call to action section
6. Footer with contact info and links
